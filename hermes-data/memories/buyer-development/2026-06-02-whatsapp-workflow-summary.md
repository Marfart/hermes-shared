## Policy / risk notes

Changing wording helps reduce obvious duplicate-message patterns, but it does not remove risk by itself.

The main risk factors are still:

- sending unsolicited messages
- sending at scale too quickly
- user blocks / reports
- repeated low-quality outreach

Best practice:

1. vary wording
2. keep messages relevant to the lead
3. keep volume controlled
4. prefer high-fit leads first
5. include only one clean website link
6. keep a record of the actual variant sent to each lead
